The Laundry Reservation System

A proprietary and premium system made by The Wash Wizards, The Laundry Reservation system is a solution to common Olin Laundry Problems.

Oliners constantly face issues with laundry. From clothes left by people to acquiring unwanted laundry times, The Laundry Reservation system is a specialized technology based solution to communicate logistics regarding laundry for a more stress free laundry experience.

  • Learn More

Efficiency

The spreadsheet system offers better communication between peers to optimize the amount of laundry completed throughout the day by providing Oliners info about their peer's laundry.

Hassle Free

Oliners are able to specify how they would like their laundry to be dealt with in order to maintain a respectful environment between laundry doers.

Simple

Our system enables Oliners to conveniently check the status of the laundry machines as well as ensure that their laundry is transferred correctly to avoid miscommunications between Oliners.
